Head Up - ECE496 Capstone

A smart, posture-aware phone holder that tracks head pose and automatically repositions using a 4-DOF robotic arm.

Overview

Head Up is a real-time, computer vision–driven system that helps reduce neck strain by keeping a smartphone aligned with a user’s head and eye position. The device uses an embedded AI board to perform face detection, facial landmark extraction, and head-pose estimation from a live camera feed. Based on the estimated head position and orientation, a 4-DOF robotic arm continuously adjusts the phone’s position and angle to maintain an ergonomic viewing posture.
